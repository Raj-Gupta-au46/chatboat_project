Understanding Your Longevity

Time is our most precious asset and defines our life journey. Our goal is to make the best of the rest of our longevity.

Babies born in developed countries are typically living about twice as long as 200 years ago. This is not because of evolution. It reflects what we have learnt since the Industrial Revolution began to significantly change our societies.

Life expectancies at birth have more than doubled in developed countries over the past 200 years. Evolution has not caused this. The Industrial Revolution caused major changes in what we have learnt and applied to managing our health better.
